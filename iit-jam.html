IIT JAM Mathematics Preparation

The Joint Admission Test for M.Sc. requires deep conceptual analytical skills rather than general calculation speed. Our course is designed to address every aspect of the JAM syllabus:

  • ✓
    Real Analysis: Sequences and series of real numbers, functions of one and two variables, Riemann integration.
  • ✓
    Linear Algebra: Vector spaces, linear transformations, matrices, eigenvalues and eigenvectors.
  • ✓
    Abstract Algebra: Groups, subgroups, normal subgroups, homomorphisms, cyclic and permutation groups.
  • ✓
    Calculus & Differential Equations: Multiple integrals, line, surface, and volume integrals, ordinary differential equations.

CSIR NET JRF - Mathematical Sciences

For candidates seeking lectureship and research fellowships. We cover complex analysis, algebra, topology, differential equations, and numerical analysis. Includes comprehensive study booklets containing solved papers of the last 15 years.
