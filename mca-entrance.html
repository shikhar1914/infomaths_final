Targeted Exams We Cover

We offer specialized batches that address the syllabus requirements of the major national MCA entrances:

NIMCET (NIT MCA Common Entrance Test)

Focuses on advanced mathematics (algebra, calculus, probability, vectors, trigonometry), logical reasoning, English comprehension, and computer fundamentals. Our NIMCET test series simulates the exact online platform with full rankings.

CUET PG (MCA Entrance)

Covers computer concepts, mathematical skills, reasoning, and verbal comprehension based on the central university common curriculum. We provide rich handouts and sample papers.

MAH MCA CET (Maharashtra CET)

A speed-oriented test focusing heavily on math, logical reasoning, and computer concepts. Our test portal helps students master speed, time allocation, and high-accuracy selections.

Panjab University (PU MCA) & Others

Preparation for PU CET PG, HCU, JNU, and other university entrances. We customize preparation according to different paper pattern variations.
